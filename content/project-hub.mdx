# ProjectHub

A platform where students can share their projects with the world. Whether you're into coding, design, engineering, or any other field, you can upload and showcase your work.

## Overview

ProjectHub connects student creators with a global audience. Share your projects, get feedback, discover what others are building, and build your portfolio.

## Key Features

- **Project Uploads** – Share projects with descriptions and media
- **Category Browsing** – Explore projects by field or technology
- **User Profiles** – Showcase your complete portfolio
- **Engagement** – Like and comment on projects
- **Search** – Find projects by tags or keywords

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React.js, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Appwrite (BaaS)
- **Hosting**: DigitalOcean
- **Deployment**: Vercel
